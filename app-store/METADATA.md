## Description

Your therapy sessions deserve the same confidentiality as your therapist's office. The Therapy Binder is a private, encrypted journaling app designed specifically for therapy notes, mood tracking, and personal reflection.

Every entry you write is encrypted on your device before it is ever saved. There is no server, no cloud sync, and no account to create. Your data stays on your phone, protected by zero-knowledge encryption that even we cannot read. Lock the app with Face ID or Touch ID for an extra layer of security.

PRIVATE JOURNALING
Write freely about your therapy sessions, breakthroughs, and challenges. Capture what your therapist said, how you felt, and what you want to work on next. Your journal is your space — completely private and always available.

MOOD TRACKING
Log your mood after each session or throughout the day. Over time, The Therapy Binder reveals patterns in how you feel, helping you and your therapist identify trends and measure progress.

PATTERN INSIGHTS
See your emotional journey at a glance. Visual summaries highlight recurring themes, mood shifts, and growth over weeks and months. Bring these insights to your next appointment to make every session count.

EXPORT TO PDF
Need to share progress with your therapist or keep a backup? Export individual sessions or your full journal to PDF. You control what gets shared and when.

BIOMETRIC LOCK
Protect your most personal thoughts with Face ID or Touch ID. The app locks automatically, so even if someone picks up your phone, your journal stays private.

BUILT FOR THERAPY
Unlike generic note apps, The Therapy Binder is structured around therapy sessions. Track session dates, therapist names, topics discussed, homework assignments, and personal reflections — all in one place.

ZERO-KNOWLEDGE PRIVACY
We believe your mental health journey is nobody's business but yours. The Therapy Binder uses end-to-end encryption with keys generated on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no data collection. Your entries never leave your phone unless you choose to export them.

Whether you are in CBT, DBT, talk therapy, or any other modality, The Therapy Binder helps you get more from every session. Start journaling today and take an active role in your mental health.
