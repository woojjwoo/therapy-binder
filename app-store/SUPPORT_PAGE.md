# The Therapy Binder — Support & FAQ

## How does encryption work?

The Therapy Binder encrypts all of your data on your device before it is saved. During initial setup, you create a passphrase that is used to generate your encryption keys. These keys never leave your device and are never sent to us. Every journal entry, mood log, voice memo, and image is encrypted using these keys. Even if someone gained access to your device's storage, they would not be able to read your entries without your passphrase. We use zero-knowledge encryption, which means we have no ability to view, access, or decrypt your data.

## What happens if I forget my passphrase?

During setup, The Therapy Binder provides you with a recovery key. If you forget your passphrase, you can use your recovery key to regain access to your data and set a new passphrase. If you lose both your passphrase and your recovery key, your data cannot be recovered. We do not store your passphrase or recovery key anywhere, so we cannot reset it for you. We strongly recommend writing down your recovery key and storing it in a safe place, such as a locked drawer or a password manager.

## Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export your journal entries to PDF at any time. You can export a single session, a range of sessions by date, or your entire journal. The PDF is generated on your device, and you can save it to your Files app, send it via email, AirDrop it, or share it however you choose. We never receive a copy of your exports. This feature is useful for sharing selected entries with your therapist or keeping a personal backup.

## Is my data backed up to iCloud?

The Therapy Binder does not use iCloud or any cloud service to store or sync your data. Your journal entries, mood data, and all other content remain on your device. If you have full-device iCloud backups enabled in your iOS settings, your entire device (including app data) may be included in that backup. This is controlled by Apple and your device settings, not by our app. If you want to ensure your therapy data is excluded from iCloud backups, you can disable iCloud Backup for your device in Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > iCloud Backup.

## How do I set up daily reminders?

Open The Therapy Binder and go to Settings. Tap on "Daily Reminder" and toggle it on. Choose the time of day you would like to be reminded to journal. The app will send you a local notification at that time each day. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external server. You can change the time or disable reminders at any time from the same settings screen.

## Is The Therapy Binder free?

The Therapy Binder offers core journaling and encryption features for free. Some advanced features, such as mood pattern insights and PDF export, are available through a subscription. You can view available plans in the app under Settings > Subscription.

## Can my therapist see my entries?

No. Your therapist cannot see your entries unless you choose to share them. The Therapy Binder has no sharing or collaboration features that would give anyone else access to your data. If you want to share something with your therapist, you can export specific sessions to PDF and share the file directly.

## How do I contact support?

If you have a question, issue, or suggestion, you can reach us at support@therapybinder.app. We typically respond within 48 hours.
