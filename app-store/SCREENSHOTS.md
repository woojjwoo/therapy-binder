# App Store Screenshots

## Required Sizes
- **iPhone 6.9"** (required): iPhone 16 Pro Max — 1320 x 2868 px
- **iPhone 6.5"** (optional but recommended): iPhone 14 Plus / 13 Pro Max — 1284 x 2778 px

## Screenshots to Capture

### 1. Welcome / Onboarding
- **Screen:** Welcome screen with app branding and "Get Started" button
- **Caption:** "Your private therapy journal"
- **Notes:** Show the clean onboarding flow. Emphasize privacy messaging.

### 2. Session Timeline
- **Screen:** Timeline view with 3-4 sample sessions showing dates, titles, and mood indicators
- **Caption:** "Track every session"
- **Notes:** Use realistic sample data. Show variety in mood colors.

### 3. New Session Entry
- **Screen:** Session editor with title, date, therapist name, and notes being typed
- **Caption:** "Capture what matters"
- **Notes:** Show a partially filled session with realistic therapy content.

### 4. Mood Tracking
- **Screen:** Mood selection or mood history view with visual indicators
- **Caption:** "Log how you feel"
- **Notes:** Show the mood picker or a filled-out mood entry.

### 5. Pattern Insights
- **Screen:** Patterns/insights view showing mood trends or session summaries over time
- **Caption:** "See your progress"
- **Notes:** Populate with enough data to show a meaningful trend line or summary.

### 6. Export to PDF
- **Screen:** Export dialog or PDF preview of a session
- **Caption:** "Share on your terms"
- **Notes:** Show the export options or a generated PDF preview.

### 7. Settings & Security
- **Screen:** Settings page showing Face ID toggle, encryption status, and app preferences
- **Caption:** "Secured with Face ID"
- **Notes:** Show Face ID enabled and encryption indicator.

### 8. Biometric Unlock
- **Screen:** Lock screen with Face ID prompt
- **Caption:** "Your eyes only"
- **Notes:** Show the app lock screen with the Face ID authentication UI.

## Screenshot Tips
- Use a clean device with full battery and no notifications in the status bar
- Use Simulator with `xcrun simctl status_bar` to set a clean status bar (9:41 AM, full signal, Wi-Fi, 100% battery)
- Consider adding marketing frames/captions around screenshots using tools like Fastlane Frameit or Screenshots Pro
- Ensure all sample data looks realistic but contains no real personal information
