# Privacy Policy

**The Therapy Binder**
**Effective Date: March 1, 2026**

## Our Commitment

The Therapy Binder is built on a simple principle: your therapy notes are yours alone. We do not collect, transmit, store, or process any of your personal data. This privacy policy exists to make that commitment explicit and legally clear.

## Data Collection

We collect zero data. Specifically:

- We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- We do not collect or transmit the content of your journal entries, mood logs, or session notes.
- We do not collect usage analytics or behavioral data.
- We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data.
- We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of online tracking.
- We do not serve advertisements or share data with advertisers.
- We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or data brokers.

## What Stays on Your Device

All of the following data is created, stored, and encrypted entirely on your device. None of it is transmitted to us or to any third party:

- Journal entries (text, voice memos, and images)
- Mood tracking data
- Session metadata (dates, therapist names, topics, homework)
- Pattern and insight summaries
- App preferences and settings
- Your encryption keys and recovery key

## Encryption

The Therapy Binder uses end-to-end encryption with keys generated locally on your device. Your encryption key is derived from a passphrase that you set during initial setup. We do not have access to your passphrase or encryption keys. We cannot decrypt your data, and we cannot recover it on your behalf. If you lose your passphrase and recovery key, your data cannot be restored by anyone, including us.

## Biometric Authentication

The Therapy Binder supports Face ID and Touch ID for convenient access. Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (iOS). Your biometric data (fingerprint or face scan) is never accessed, stored, or processed by The Therapy Binder. We simply request authentication through Apple's LocalAuthentication framework, which returns a success or failure result without exposing biometric data to our app.

## iCloud and Cloud Storage

The Therapy Binder does not use iCloud, CloudKit, or any cloud storage service. Your data is not backed up to iCloud unless you have enabled full-device iCloud backups through your iOS settings, which is controlled by Apple and your device preferences, not by our app.

## PDF Export

When you export your journal to PDF, the file is generated locally on your device. You control where the file is saved or shared. We do not receive a copy of exported files.

## Notifications

If you enable daily journal reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device using iOS local notifications. No data is sent to a remote server to deliver these notifications.

## Children's Privacy

The Therapy Binder is rated 12+ and does not knowingly collect any data from children or adults. Since we collect no data from any user, we are compliant with COPPA and similar regulations by design.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at https://therapybinder.app/privacy and update the effective date. Because we collect no data, changes to this policy would relate only to clarifications or the addition of new features that maintain our zero-collection approach.

## Contact

**Email:** privacy@therapybinder.app

## Summary

We do not collect your data. We cannot read your journal. We do not track you. Your therapy notes stay on your device, encrypted with keys only you control. That is our entire privacy policy.
